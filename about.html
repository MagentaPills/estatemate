About EstateMate

EstateMate is your AI real estate co-pilot. We’re here to get you from “I’m just browsing” to “I’m confident signing” a lot faster — and with a lot less spammy phone calls.

Ask us anything in plain English. “Show me modern townhouses with parking under 2M AED.” “What’s the rental yield on this 1BR in Marina?” “Which areas are actually quiet if I work from home?”

We surface listings that match, highlight pros/cons, and prep you for next steps like viewing, paperwork, and budgeting. No fluff — just clarity.
